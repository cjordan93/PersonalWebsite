Hello, I am a recent college graduate with a degree in Computer Science. Since graduation, I have shifted my focus to front-end development. I want to create websites that users would love to visit. I beleive that to achieve that a website must be aestheically pleasing, simple to navigate, and have clear indications of what a user can do. For indications, this can be as simple as changing a link's color or opacity on a hover state. I am enjoying digging into my creative side, and cannot wait to see where my journey will take me.
